### NARRATIVE STRUCTURE (FRAME STORY)

**El marco narrativo (Frame narrative):**

1. **Opening:** Count Lucanor has a problem
2. **Consultation:** He asks his advisor Patronio for advice
3. **Tale:** Patronio tells a relevant story
4. **Application:** Patronio explains how the story applies
5. **Moral:** Don Juan Manuel summarizes in verse (pareados)
