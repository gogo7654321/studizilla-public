## ÔøΩüìã Unit Overview

The 20th century began with the catastrophe of World War I, which destroyed empires, killed millions, and shattered the optimism of the previous era. The unstable peace that followed produced economic crisis, totalitarian regimes, and eventually an even more destructive second world war. The Holocaust revealed the depths of human cruelty. By 1945, Europe lay in ruins, overshadowed by the United States and Soviet Union! ‚öîÔ∏è
