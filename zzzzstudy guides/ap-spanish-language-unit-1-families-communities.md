### EL COMPADRAZGO (THE GODPARENT SYSTEM)

One of the most important community institutions in Latin America:

**Definition:** System of ritual kinship created through religious ceremonies

**Key relationships:**
- **Padrino/Madrina**: Godfather/Godmother - sponsors child at baptism
- **Ahijado/Ahijada**: Godson/Goddaughter - the sponsored child
- **Compadre/Comadre**: The relationship between godparents and biological parents

**Cultural significance:**
- Creates extended family networks
- Provides support system for children
- Strengthens community bonds
- Often involves financial obligations (education, gifts)
- Spiritual responsibility for child's religious upbringing

**Example vocabulary:**
- Ser padrino de boda = to be a wedding sponsor
- El compadrazgo polÃ­tico = political networking through godparent relationships
- "Mi compadre" = term of endearment/respect between adults
