## ðŸŒ¸ MODERNISMO: The Aesthetic Revolution

### Defining Characteristics

**RubÃ©n DarÃ­o's Aesthetic Revolution:**

```

   - Internal rhyme and alliteration

   - Blending of senses
   - Colors that sound, sounds that have color
   - Rich sensory imagery

5. SYMBOLISM (Simbolismo)
   - Swan (purity, aristocratic beauty)
   - Blue (ideal, infinite, dream)
   - Peacock (vanity, art)
   - Lotus (exotic spirituality)
```
