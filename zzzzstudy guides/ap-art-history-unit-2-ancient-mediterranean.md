```
- Elongated skulls
- Soft bellies
- Androgynous pharaoh

DATE: ~1323 BCE

SIGNIFICANCE:

```

```
- Imitate marble blocks
- No figures
- Decorative patterns

STYLE II (Architectural):
- Illusionistic space
- Architecture depicted
- Views through walls

- Flat, decorative
- Framed panels
- Less illusion

STYLE IV (Intricate):
- Combines elements
- Fantasy architecture
- Theatrical effects
- Vettii example

ALEXANDER MOSAIC:

DATE: ~100 BCE
```
