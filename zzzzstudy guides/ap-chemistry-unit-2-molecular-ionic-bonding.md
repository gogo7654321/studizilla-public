### Lattice Energy ğŸ’

Energy released when gaseous ions form solid crystal:

$$M^+(g) + X^-(g) \rightarrow MX(s) + \text{energy}$$

**Factors affecting lattice energy**:

| Factor | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Higher charge | Higher lattice energy |
| Smaller ions | Higher lattice energy |

**Example comparison**:
- NaCl vs MgO: MgO has higher lattice energy (2+ and 2- charges)
- NaCl vs KCl: NaCl has higher lattice energy (Naâº smaller than Kâº)
