## ÔøΩüìã Unit Overview

The late 20th and early 21st centuries have witnessed unprecedented global interconnection. Technology, trade, migration, and ideas flow across borders at speeds unimaginable to previous generations. But globalization has also brought challenges: terrorism, environmental crisis, economic inequality, and cultural conflicts. Understanding our interconnected world is essential! üåê

### Essential Questions

| Question | Focus |
|----------|-------|

| What are globalization's benefits and costs? | Trade vs. inequality |
| How do global challenges require global solutions? | Environment, disease, terrorism |
| How do people resist globalization? | Nationalism, fundamentalism |
