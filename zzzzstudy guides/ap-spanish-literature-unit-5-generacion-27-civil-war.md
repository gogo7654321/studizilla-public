### Understanding the Romance Form

**Traditional Spanish Ballad:**

```
ROMANCE CHARACTERISTICS:

Meter:
- Octosyllabic lines (8 syllables)
- Assonant rhyme in even lines
- Odd lines unrhymed (blank)

Origins:
- Medieval epic fragments
- Oral tradition
- "Romancero viejo" (old ballad tradition)

Lorca's Innovation:
- Traditional form + modern imagery
- Narrative + lyrical fusion
- Universal themes through gypsy lens
```
