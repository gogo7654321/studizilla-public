## ÔøΩüìã Unit Overview

The Renaissance ("rebirth") began in Italy and transformed European culture through a rediscovery of classical Greek and Roman learning. This cultural flowering produced new art, literature, and ways of thinking that would challenge medieval traditions. The period also saw Europeans explore the world, establishing the foundations of global trade networks and colonization! üé®
