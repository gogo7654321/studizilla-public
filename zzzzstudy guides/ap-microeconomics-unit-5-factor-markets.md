## âœ… Profit Maximizing Hiring Rule

### The Rule

| Rule | MRP = MRC |
|------|-----------|
| **If MRP > MRC** | Hire more workers |

## ğŸ­ Monopsony

### Definition

| Concept | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Monopsony** | Single buyer of labor |

### Monopsony vs. Competition

| Feature | Competition | Monopsony |
|---------|-------------|-----------|

### Why MRC > Wage in Monopsony

| Reason | Explanation |
|--------|-------------|
| **To hire more** | Must raise wage |
| **Raise for all** | Not just new worker |
| **MRC includes** | Wage + increase for existing |

### Monopsony Outcome

| Compared to Competition | Result |
|------------------------|--------|
| **Wage** | Lower |
| **Employment** | Lower |
| **Exploitation** | Workers paid less than MRP |

### The Bilateral Monopoly Case

When a union faces a monopsony employer:
- Union pushes for higher wages (upward)
- Monopsony pushes for lower wages (downward)
- Outcome depends on bargaining power
- Can actually INCREASE employment toward competitive level!
