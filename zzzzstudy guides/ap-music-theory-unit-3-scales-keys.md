## ğŸ“ Key Takeaways

```
1. MAJOR SCALE IS THE FOUNDATION
   - W W H W W W H pattern
   - All other scales relate to it
   - Key signatures derived from it

2. MINOR HAS THREE FORMS
   - Natural: Pure minor sound
   - Harmonic: Creates leading tone
   - Melodic: Smooth voice leading

3. RELATIVE KEYS SHARE NOTES
   - Same key signature
   - Different tonic
   - Down minor 3rd for relative minor

4. MODES ARE ROTATED SCALES
   - Same notes, different starting point
   - Each has unique character
   - Very important in jazz and pop

5. CIRCLE OF FIFTHS ORGANIZES ALL
   - Keys related by fifth
   - Clockwise = add sharps
   - Counter-clockwise = add flats
```
