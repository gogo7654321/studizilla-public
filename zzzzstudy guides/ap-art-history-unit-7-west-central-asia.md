### Aniconism

```
ANICONISM IN ISLAMIC ART:

PRINCIPLE:
- Avoid idolatry
- No images in mosques
- Qur'an not illustrated

BUT:
- Secular contexts = images
- Persian/Mughal painting
- No absolute prohibition
- Regional variation

SOLUTIONS:
1. Calligraphy elevated
2. Geometric patterns
3. Arabesque vegetation
4. Architecture emphasized

CONTEXT MATTERS:
Religious = no figural
Secular = figural allowed
Regional = variation
```
