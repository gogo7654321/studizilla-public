## üß† Political Culture

### Definition and Components

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Definition** | Shared values, beliefs, attitudes about politics |
| **Cognitive** | What people know |
| **Affective** | How people feel |
| **Evaluative** | How people judge |

### Types of Political Culture

| Type | Characteristics |
|------|-----------------|
| **Parochial** | Low awareness; low participation |
| **Subject** | Aware but passive; obey |
| **Participant** | Active; engaged citizens |
| **Civic culture** | Mix of subject + participant |

| Dimension | Range |
|-----------|-------|

| **Efficacy** | High ‚Üî Low |
| **Nationalism** | Strong ‚Üî Weak |
| **Tolerance** | High ‚Üî Low |
| **Democracy support** | High ‚Üî Low |

| Cleavage | Examples |
|----------|----------|

|---------|-------------|
