## ÔøΩüìã Unit Overview

The late 19th century saw the triumph of nationalism as Germany and Italy unified while empires like Austria-Hungary and the Ottomans struggled. European powers scrambled to divide Africa and dominate Asia, creating global empires justified by racism and Social Darwinism. Meanwhile, new cultural movements challenged rationalism and realism. The stage was being set for the catastrophe of World War I! üåç
