## ğŸ”„ Comparative and Absolute Advantage

### Absolute Advantage

| Concept | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Definition** | Produce more with same resources |
| **OR** | Same with fewer resources |
| **Not basis for trade** | Comparative advantage is |

### Comparative Advantage

| Concept | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Definition** | Lower opportunity cost |
| **Basis for trade** | Specialize in what you do best |
| **Everyone has one** | Even if no absolute advantage |

### Gains from Trade

| Concept | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Specialization** | Produce what you do best |
| **Trade** | Exchange for other goods |
| **Both gain** | Higher total output |
| **Terms of trade** | Between opportunity costs |

## ğŸ“Š Comparative Advantage Calculation

### Step-by-Step Method

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| **1** | Find maximum output for each country/person |
| **2** | Calculate opportunity cost for each good |
| **3** | Compare OCâ€”lower OC = comparative advantage |
| **4** | Each should specialize in lower OC good |
| **5** | Terms of trade: between the two OCs |

### Example Problem

| Country | Max Wheat | Max Cloth |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| **USA** | 100 | 50 |
| **UK** | 60 | 40 |

| Country | OC of 1 Wheat | OC of 1 Cloth |
|---------|---------------|---------------|
| **USA** | 50/100 = 0.5 Cloth | 100/50 = 2 Wheat |
| **UK** | 40/60 = 0.67 Cloth | 60/40 = 1.5 Wheat |

**Result:** USA has CA in Wheat (0.5 < 0.67), UK has CA in Cloth (1.5 < 2)

**Terms of Trade:** 1 Wheat should trade for between 0.5 and 0.67 Cloth

## ğŸ“ FRQ Practice

### Sample Free Response Question

**Question:** Country A can produce 100 units of food or 50 units of clothing. Country B can produce 80 units of food or 40 units of clothing.

**(a)** Which country has an absolute advantage in food? In clothing?

**(b)** Calculate the opportunity cost of producing 1 unit of food for each country.

**(c)** Which country has a comparative advantage in food? Explain.

**(d)** If the countries specialize and trade, what would be an acceptable terms of trade for 1 unit of food?

### Model Answer

**(a)** Country A has absolute advantage in BOTH goods (100 > 80 for food; 50 > 40 for clothing)

**(b)** 
- Country A: OC of 1 food = 50/100 = **0.5 clothing**
- Country B: OC of 1 food = 40/80 = **0.5 clothing**

**(c)** Neither has comparative advantage in foodâ€”OC is equal (0.5 = 0.5). When opportunity costs are equal, there are no gains from trade in this model.

**(d)** No mutually beneficial terms exist since opportunity costs are identical.
