## ğŸ“– DON QUIJOTE DE LA MANCHA: OVERVIEW

### THE FIRST MODERN NOVEL

**Why is Don Quijote considered the first modern novel?**

| Innovation | Explanation |
|------------|-------------|
| **Psychological depth** | Characters evolve and have inner lives |
| **Self-awareness** | Novel comments on itself and literature |
| **Multiple perspectives** | Reality is questioned, not fixed |
| **Complex irony** | Multiple layers of meaning |
| **Realism + fantasy** | Blend of everyday and imaginary |
| **Character development** | Don Quijote and Sancho change |

### THE PLOT (SUMMARY)

**Part I:**
1. Alonso Quijano reads too many chivalric romances
2. He goes mad and believes himself a knight errant
3. He renames himself Don Quijote de la Mancha
4. He chooses Aldonza Lorenzo as his lady (Dulcinea del Toboso)
5. First sally: alone, dubbed "knight" at an inn
6. Second sally: with Sancho Panza as squire
7. Famous adventures: windmills, flocks, galley slaves
8. Brought home in a cage by friends

**Part II:**
1. Don Quijote and Sancho know they're famous (from Part I)
2. Third sally with increased self-awareness
3. Stay with Duke and Duchess (who mock them)
4. Sancho "governs" the island of Barataria
5. Defeat by Knight of the White Moon (SansÃ³n Carrasco)
6. Return home, regains sanity, dies
